# Chrome Web Store Listing

## Name
YouTube Transcript Reader

## Short Description (132 chars max)
Read YouTube transcripts without clicking "Show transcript". Search, copy, and click timestamps to jump in the video.

## Detailed Description
Skip the hassle of YouTube's hidden transcript button. This extension shows the full transcript right next to the video - always visible, always searchable.

**Perfect for:**
• Students taking notes from lectures
• Researchers finding specific quotes
• Language learners reading along
• Content creators analyzing videos
• Anyone who reads faster than they listen

**Key Features:**
• Instant transcript access - no digging through menus
• Search with highlighting - find any word instantly
• Click-to-seek - tap any line to jump there
• Timeline view - visual chips that follow along as you watch
• Copy or download - get the text however you need it

**Clean & Private:**
No account needed. No data collected. Just works.
